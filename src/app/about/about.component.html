Our Story & Philosophy

Samarth Mahila Sankalp Foundation:
Empowering Rural Women, Building a Stronger India

SMS Foundation is a pioneering social organization committed to transforming lives through comprehensive health security initiatives, sustainable wealth management solutions, and impactful social welfare programs. Our mission centers on creating a self-reliant society by making essential healthcare products accessible and affordable.

Founded with a deep commitment to women's empowerment and community welfare, SMS Foundation operates across India, connecting thousands of families with quality healthcare and financial support systems. We believe that every woman deserves access to dignified healthcare and economic opportunity.

आज़ादी भी स्त्रीलिंग है | Freedom is Feminine
